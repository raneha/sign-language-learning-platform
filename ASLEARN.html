Welcome to the ASL Learning Platform!

Our interactive platform helps users learn American Sign Language (ASL) through real-time gesture recognition.
The system processes hand gestures from a webcam, identifies ASL signs, & provides instant feedback.
With a simple, user-friendly interface & an engaging scoring system, we make learning ASL fun & accessible for everyone.
